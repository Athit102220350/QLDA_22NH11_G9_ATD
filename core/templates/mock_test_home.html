Mock Test Practice

Simulate real English proficiency tests to assess your skills and track your progress.

Each test includes three sections:

  • Reading Comprehension: Read passages and answer multiple-choice questions
  • Grammar: Fill in the blanks with the correct grammatical forms
  • Vocabulary: Identify synonyms and antonyms of given words

After completing a test, you'll receive:

  • Your overall score and CEFR level (A1-C2)
  • Performance breakdown by section
  • Personalized study recommendations
CEFR Levels

Your score will determine your CEFR level:

  • A1 Beginner (0-30%)
  • A2 Elementary (31-50%)
  • B1 Intermediate (51-70%)
  • B2 Upper-Intermediate (71-85%)
  • C1 Advanced (86-95%)
  • C2 Proficiency (96-100%)
